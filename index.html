ABOUT US

We love Space

Here at KASA we will build low-cost autonomous satellites to collect and recycle space debris in the earth's orbit. As our reliance on satellites and other space-based technologies continues to grow, so does the amount of debris orbiting around Earth. We chose this idea because we are reliant on operational satellites that are used in day to day applications, like GPS, broadcasting, research and communication. Therefore, it is essential to prevent space debris from colliding with these satellites. Recycling space debris will increase the efficiency and sustainability of our space operations and can be utilized to build new satellites and other equipment.

According to NASA, there are more than 170 million separate particles of space debris larger than 1 mm. Our space debris clean up service will work with satellite operators and space agencies to remove space debris and bring them back to earth to be recycled. The recycled materials would be used for the production of new parts for the satellites and other machinery.
